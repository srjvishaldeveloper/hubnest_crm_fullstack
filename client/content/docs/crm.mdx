### CRM Core Modules

The CRM module allows tenants to manage contacts, track deal status, schedule activities, and optimize sales funnels.

#### 1. Lead & Pipeline Management
Leads represent potential customers. They flow through customizable pipelines:
- **Lead Capture**: Automated via web API endpoints or manual imports.
- **Kanban Pipeline**: Drag-and-drop lead boards (New, Qualified, Proposal, Negotiation, Won, Lost).
- **Lead Scoring**: AI-assisted prioritization highlighting contacts with highest conversion indicators.

#### 2. Contacts & Accounts
- **Contacts**: Individual profile records storing phone, email, and activity timeline.
- **Accounts**: B2B customer organizations mapping to multiple contacts.

#### 3. Deals & Forecasting
Deals store transaction values and close-date forecasts:
- Automated revenue forecast calculation on the Finance dashboard.
- Historic sales conversion analytics per representative.

#### 4. Activities, Tasks & Calendar
Track sales representatives' actions:
- **Tasks**: Priority checklists with due dates.
- **Calendar Integration**: Synchronizes meetings with Google Calendar and Outlook.
