### Support Desk Overview

Maintain strong customer relations using HubNest CRM's built-in Support Desk modules.

#### 1. Ticket Management
Tickets group customer issues, tracking priority and assignment:
- **Automatic Intake**: Converts incoming support emails (configured via IMAP integration) into tickets.
- **Priority Matrix**: Low, Medium, High, and Urgent severity tags.
- **Assignee Routing**: Directs tickets to available Support Agents depending on their active workload.

#### 2. SLA Management
Define Service Level Agreements (SLA) to enforce reply deadlines:
- **First Response Time**: Target time (e.g., within 2 hours for Urgent tickets).
- **Resolution Time**: Limit to mark tickets as Resolved.
- **Escalation Triggers**: Warns Support Managers via email if ticket SLAs are at risk of breach.

#### 3. Knowledge Base
Create help directories linked to the public `/help-center` path:
- Markdown-based articles categorized by module.
- Search engine optimization (SEO) indexing on public articles.
