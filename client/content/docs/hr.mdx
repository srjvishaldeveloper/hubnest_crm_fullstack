### HR Core Features

HubNest CRM integrates operational Human Resource (HR) modules directly inside the platform:

#### 1. Employee Directory
Manage staff data, roles, and profiles. Each employee maps to a specific workspace and department (CRM, Marketing, Support, Finance, HR).

#### 2. Attendance & Check-In
- **Dynamic Check-in**: GPS or IP-restricted check-in buttons inside the user dashboard.
- **Monthly Timesheets**: Exportable attendance summaries mapping working hours and delay metrics.

#### 3. Leave Management
Allows staff to submit leave requests (Vacation, Sick Leave, Unpaid Leave):
1. **Request Submission**: Employee submits a request with dates and reasons.
2. **Review Workflow**: HR managers and Department heads are notified to approve/reject.
3. **Balance Tracker**: Automated balance updates mapping remaining annual allowances.

#### 4. Recruitment & Careers
Post public openings (linked to the `/careers` page) and manage applicants:
- Track application stages (Review, Interview, Assessment, Offered, Hired).
- Internal feedback scoring per interviewer.
